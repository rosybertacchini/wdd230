Reserving a Scoot at Coastal Scoot Rentals couldn't be simpler



Before proceeding with your scooter rental, please review the following terms and conditions:


Rental Duration:

Your rental period begins on the selected start date and time and ends on the designated end date and time. Extension of the rental period is subject to availability and must be approved in advance by Coastal Scoot Rentals.

Secure Deposit:

A security deposit of $200 is required upon rental to cover potential damages or losses. The deposit will be refunded upon the safe return of the scooter, minus any applicable deductions.

Renter Responsibilities:

Operate the scooter responsibly, adhering to all local traffic laws and regulations. Assume responsibility for any damages caused by misuse or negligence. Do not sublease or transfer the scooter without prior consent from Coastal Scoot Rentals.

Insurance:

Basic insurance coverage is provided with the rental. Additional insurance options are available for purchase to reduce liability in case of accidents or damages.
