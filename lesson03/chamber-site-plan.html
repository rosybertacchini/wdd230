Tijuana Chamber Of Commerce Site Plan

Rosy Bertacchini

WDD 230

Site Name

Tijuana Chamber of Commerce - CANACO

Site Purpose

The pupose of this site is to serves as a business organization that aims to promote and advocate for the interests of the local business community. Its purpose is to facilitate economic growth, enhance the business environment, and support the overall prosperity of the community of Tijuana,BC in Mexico.

Scenarios

  1. Are there upcoming networking events or business gatherings organized by the chamber where I can connect with other local businesses?
  2. What resources and support does the chamber of commerce provide to help me start and grow my business in this community?
  3. What resources and support does the chamber of commerce provide to help me start and grow my business in this community?
  4. What are the benefits of becoming a member of the chamber of commerce, and how can my business take advantage of these perks?
  5. How does the chamber of commerce advocate for local businesses in terms of government policies, and what resources are available for understanding regulatory changes affecting my industry?
